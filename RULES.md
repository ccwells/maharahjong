# Maharahjong Rules v0.1 - Complete Draft

## Introduction
Maharahjong is a beautiful fusion of the best elements from Chinese Mahjong (strategic melds and flexible hands), Japanese Riichi Mahjong (ready declarations and defensive play), American Mahjong (social Charleston passing and evolving hands), and Indian cultural traditions (Rummy-style runs, rangoli artistry, Vedic symbols, and festival storytelling).

The goal is a premium, unique game that feels familiar yet distinctly Indian - vibrant, strategic, social, and culturally resonant.

**Players:** 4 (ideal for family and friends)
**Tiles:** 152
**Objective:** Form 4 melds + 1 pair (14 tiles) or special thematic hands to declare "Maharajah!" (Mahjong).

## Tile Set

## Setup
1. Build the wall: 34 stacks of 2 tiles (68 stacks total for wall).
2. Each player receives 13 tiles.
3. Determine the dealer (East wind position rotates or is chosen).
4. Optional: Place a small "kitty" pot for side bets or bonuses (Indian club tradition).

## The Charleston (Social Warm-up - American Influence)
At the start of each round:
- Pass 3 tiles to the right.
- Pass 3 tiles across the table.
- Pass 3 tiles to the left.
- Optional courtesy pass (1 tile each way).

This adds social interaction and fun, like Indian card game passing.

## Core Gameplay (Chinese/Japanese Base with Indian Twists)
1. **Draw** a tile from the wall or claim a discard (with priority rules).
2. **Form Melds** (can be claimed from discards):
   - **Pung**: 3 identical tiles.
   - **Kong**: 4 identical tiles (draw replacement tile).
   - **Chow/Run**: 3 consecutive tiles in the same suit (Rummy-style, encouraged for Indian flavor).
3. **Discard** one tile face up.
4. **Declare "Ready"** (Riichi-style): If your hand is one tile away from winning and no open melds, declare "Ready" for bonus points and to lock your hand (adds strategy and defense).
5. **Win** by completing 4 melds + 1 pair (or special hand) and declaring "Maharajah!"

**Claiming Priority:** Maharajah > Kong > Pung > Chow.

## Special Indian Rules & Bonuses
- **Rangoli Flowers**: Reveal for bonus points + draw a replacement tile. Match your seat "wind" or current festival for double points.
- **Chakra Wilds**: Use up to 2-3 per hand for substituting in runs or sets (represents balance and energy).
- **Festival Hands**: Special scoring for thematic combinations (e.g., all-peacock suit for grace, rangoli bouquet for beauty).
- **Storytelling Melds**: Bonus for melds that "tell a story" (e.g., epic-inspired patterns) - optional house rule.

## Winning Hands
- Standard: 4 melds + pair.
- Special: All one suit, concealed hand, ready declaration win, full rangoli bouquet, etc.
- Indian Twist: Rummy-style "runs heavy" hands score extra.

## Scoring (Hybrid System)
- Base points for melds and pair.
- Fan/doubling for patterns (like Chinese/Japanese).
- Flower bonuses and festival multipliers (Indian flair).
- Ready declaration bonus.
- Self-draw bonus.
- Cultural multipliers (e.g., Diwali round double).

Full point table and examples will be refined in playtesting.

## Game Flow & Rounds
- Play multiple rounds until a target score or agreed number.
- Dealer rotates or wind changes.
- Optional: Use a card for seasonal special hands (American influence) updated for Indian festivals.

## Etiquette & Fun
- Announce melds clearly.
- Keep discards neat for defense (Riichi style).
- Enjoy the social aspect - chat, laugh, tell stories like Indian card nights.
- Respect the "click-clack" rhythm of the tiles.
