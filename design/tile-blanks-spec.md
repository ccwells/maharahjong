## Material Comparison

| Property | Acrylic | Melamine | Acrylic + Wood Back |
|----------|---------|----------|---------------------|
| Weight feel | Light-medium | Medium | Medium-heavy (premium) |
| Surface for UV printing | Excellent | Good (test adhesion) | Excellent (acrylic face) |
| Surface for laser engraving | Excellent (cold process) | Good | Excellent (acrylic face) |
| Scratch resistance | Moderate (gloss coat helps) | High | Moderate (face) / High (back) |
| Premium feel | Modern/luxury | Classic/traditional | High-end/artisanal |
| Multi-layer edges | Yes (colored layers) | Yes (colored layers) | Natural wood grain edge |
| Sound when shuffled | Light click | Solid click (traditional) | Warm, muted click |
| Cost | $$ | $ | $$$ |

### Recommendation
**Primary**: Acrylic with multi-layer colored edges (ivory face + navy or teal edge) from Jayi or Promlogo — modern, premium, excellent for both UV printing and laser engraving.
**Alternative**: Acrylic + wood back from Promlogo for an artisanal variant that combines modern tile face with traditional warmth.
**For prototyping**: Amazon GUSTARIA blanks for immediate workflow testing.
