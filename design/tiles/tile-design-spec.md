# Maharahjong Tile Design Spec

## Overview
152 total tiles. Each tile face is designed for a premium physical mahjong tile (32×23×13mm per tile, face is 32×23mm).

## Art Direction
Derived from the moodboard: temple stone carvings, vibrant rangoli floor art, peacock motifs, kalamkari textile patterns, and luxury mahjong sets.

- **Style**: Clean vector line art with rich fills. Inspired by rangoli stencils and Hoysala temple relief — intricate but readable at tile size.
- **Color palette**: Deep teal/navy background, saffron gold, magenta/hot pink, emerald green, royal purple, marigold orange. Gold metallic line work throughout.
- **Tile body**: Ivory/cream face with a thin gold border. Suit color coding for quick recognition during play.
- **Number indicator**: Small numeral (1-9) in a decorative cartouche at the top-left corner of each suited tile.
- **Consistency**: All tiles share the same border treatment, background texture (subtle rangoli dot pattern), and gold filigree corner accents.

## Production Notes
- Generate each tile design at **2048×1536px minimum** (4:3 ratio matching tile face proportions).
- Export both full-color and gold-on-white outline variants (the outline version is useful for print-and-play / prototyping).
- Maintain consistent margins — the design should have ~5% padding from tile edge to art boundary.
- The three suits should be instantly distinguishable by color even at arm's length during play: pink (Lotus), teal (Peacock), purple (Vedic).
- Honor tiles get a thicker gold border to visually separate them from suited tiles.
- Rangoli/Chakra bonus tiles get a scalloped or iridescent border treatment for instant recognition.
